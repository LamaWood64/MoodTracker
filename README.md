# Mood Tracker 

Mood Tracker is my very first iOS application, created due to the lack of simple and efficient mood-tracking solutions on the market.

## Overview

The app is designed with a clean, minimal, and intuitive user interface that makes mood logging effortless.

Instead of spending 30–40 seconds writing detailed notes about your emotional state, users can log their mood in under 8 seconds by simply selecting their current state on a scale.

The application automatically collects and organizes this data, allowing users to track their emotional patterns over time — daily, weekly, and monthly.

## 📈 Key Features

- Fast mood logging (1–5 scale input)
- Minimal and user-friendly UI
- Automatic mood history tracking
- Progress visualization over time
- Insights into emotional patterns across different life periods

##  My purpose

The goal of the app is to help users better understand their emotional well-being by identifying correlations between mood changes and external factors in daily life.

Even a few seconds of input can generate long-term, meaningful insights into personal mental patterns.

## Tech Stack

- Swift
- SwiftUI
- Xcode
- AI power: Chat GPT and DeepSeek

## Vision

This project focuses on simplicity, consistency, and usability — turning a habit that usually feels tedious into a fast, seamless interaction.

Over time, the collected data helps users reflect on their emotional state and better understand what influences their mood.
